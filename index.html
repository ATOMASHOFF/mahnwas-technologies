Product — 01
Gym Management
Gym OS
Smart gym management for fitness businesses

A comprehensive digital platform built to modernize how fitness businesses run. Gym OS replaces manual registers, WhatsApp reminders, and spreadsheet chaos with one unified system for attendance, memberships, and revenue tracking.

  • QR-based attendance with real-time member logs
  • Membership lifecycle, renewals, and plan management
  • Revenue analytics and monthly financial reporting
  • Member profile management and communication tools
Target — Gyms, fitness studios, and training centers

Future Platforms
Concept Project — In Research
HealthyFi
Digital Health Record Platform

HealthyFi is a conceptual digital health record platform designed to help individuals and families securely store medical data — prescriptions, test results, diagnoses, and doctor notes — in one accessible timeline. The goal is to eliminate lost medical history and make healthcare data portable across providers.

Lifetime Medical Storage
Encrypted, persistent storage for prescriptions, lab reports, and diagnoses — accessible at any time, from any device.
Personal Health Timeline
A chronological view of medical history giving patients and doctors full context on every consultation.
Family Record Management
Manage health data for the entire household — individual profiles with controlled sharing across family members.
