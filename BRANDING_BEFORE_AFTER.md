# 🎯 BRANDING CHANGES - BEFORE & AFTER

## Website Content Updates

Here's exactly how the website messaging has changed:

---

## 1️⃣ HERO SECTION

### BEFORE:
```

gyms, educational institutes, and small businesses manage operations digitally."
```
